Our Story

Founded with a vision to revolutionize the heavy-duty vehicle industry, SJ Motors has grown from a small manufacturing unit to one of India's most trusted names in commercial vehicles.

Our partnership with Tata Motors enables us to deliver world-class Hyva, Signa, and Dost series vehicles that meet the demanding requirements of construction, logistics, and mining industries.
